Branding & Gift Items

BRANDING HAS NEVER BEEN THIS EASY AND SIMPLE!

Noxie Limited is a leading a supplier of promotional items and corporate gifts to leading brands in the Nigerian and West African market. We specialize on items used for corporate branding and all forms of items below the line advertising. Our focus has always been and will continue to be, on offering our customers great service, competitive pricing, swift delivery and a wide range of products. We are also a leading manufacturer and supplier of specialized corporate clothing and work wear garments in Nigeria. We pride ourselves in delivering top quality goods in the fastest possible time at the best available price. We have an extensive range of clothing to suit each individual company's needs. We have a wide range of stylish and trendy uniform line designed using the finest quality fabrics which apart from school uniforms also include men's workwear and women's workwear. Our products are available in a wide range of colors, designs and styles and we also customize our products

OFFICE AND LESIURE FURNITURE SUPPLY

OCCUPY YOUR SPACE WITH STYLE!

Since 2001, Noxie Limited has been transforming work and home environments into corporate and living spaces that are classy, chic, homely, and light-filled. We are very flexible and responsive and able to create a unique style that is totally in tune with your taste. We also provide fixtures, fittings, fabrics and high-end electronics to complement your home or office. We maintain a team of very experienced and reliable workmen ranging from bricklayers, carpenters, plumbers and cabinet makers.

Haulage & Tug Boats

LET US GET YOU THERE!

We have the infrastructure, resources and experience to provide supply chain solutions that enhance the customer's brand values and align with their strategic objectives. Whether it is a single pallet or a complex logistics solution, our customers receive the benefit of our expertise, skill, dedication and professionalism. We have a wide range of transport and haulage solutions whether chilled, frozen, ambient or containerized. Our commitment to a high standard of customer satisfaction with exceptional, safe, quality service distinguishes us from other marine transportation companies. The core principles for our barge and tugboat leasing business are Health, Safety and Environment which are reflected in our business relationships with our employees and our clients. We conduct our business in a responsible and ethical manner in order to protect the environment and shall continue to drive sustainable growth within the company; whilst adhering to the highest standards of health, safety and environmental practices. We are committed to protecting the health and safety of all stakeholders in our operations; including our employees, our customers, our vendors and the public.

Personal Protective Equipments (PPE)

GET PROTECTED!

We offer an extensive range of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) & Workwear to satisfy the most demanding of safety requirements. We provide an assorted range of PPE and Workwear which includes Safety Footwear (safety shoes, safety boots, rigger boots, waterproof footwear etc), Respiratory Protection (respiratory masks, full face and half-face masks etc), Hand Protection (builders grip gloves, rigger gloves, disposable gloves and cut-resistant gloves) and Safety Head Wear among others.
